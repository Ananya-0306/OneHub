EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW

Frequently asked questions

Can I trust Zabal Media?


Absolutely! We are vetted by Webflow as one of their trusted enterprise level partners. Being an enterprise partner with Webflow is the highest honor for any agency using Webflow to build websites.

Lastly, we are all practitioners. Before Zabal Media was founded, the founder Wilian worked at two startups that were acquired by Google (Kifi & Cronologics). Wilian went on to work At Google as a lead product designer where he helped successful teams strategize, design, develop, launch, and iterate at scale. Zabal Media is now applying these practices with our partners.

Can we get training on how to use Webflow?


Definitely. At the end of each project, we do a recorded zoom call where we do a high level overview of Webflow as a platform.

Things we cover:

  • How to use the Webflow editor?
  • How to use the Webflow designer?
  • How to maintain the Webflow CMS?
  • How do all project settings work?
  • How to apply SEO best practices into your project?
  • And much more..…
Book a Webflow training call
What if we need help after the website is launched?


You’re in good hands. We look for ongoing partnerships that are built on trust, value exchange, and growth. We collaborate with a few of our partners on an ongoing basis as an extension of their team. We use your communication tools, project management tools, etc. Or we can do one-off smaller projects as needed. In any case, we are here for you.

How much should I expect to spend on a website?


The real question is, “how much should you invest to get the results you’re looking for?”. The cost of a project is greatly impacted by the scope of work.

Things to consider:

  • When does the project need to go live?
  • How many pages will the website have for version one?
  • Are there any 3rd party integrations (ex: Hubspot, Marketo, Segment etc.) that need to be part of the website experience?
  • How much animation work will need to be included in the project?
  • Will you need help with design & Webflow development? Or only one?
  • Are the website designs ready to be developed?
